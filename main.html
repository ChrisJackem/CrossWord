Welcome to CrossWord

This is a simple crossword app for all browsers. You play by dragging the letters on the left/top panel into the squares in the middle.


Also on the left/top panel are the clues. The numbers correspond to the numbers in the middle grid, across and down.


Once all the letters are placed in the correct places, you will complete the level. See if you can beat them all !
